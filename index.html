Travel Information

Important

  • Every person traveling will need a valid passport. If you do not have a passport, you should apply for one ASAP as the process can take months. Find more information here.
  • Your passport should not expire within 6 months AFTER travel! Even though your passport may be valid, the airline can deny you boarding if your passport expires within 6 months AFTER travel. Do not risk it, renew if you are due!

Frequently Asked Questions

Question

What is the weather like in Cabo in April?

Answer

Upper 70's to mid 80's during the day, with sunshine of course!

At night it can get quite windy and chilly so pack accordingly!

Question

What time is check-in and check-out?

Answer

Check in time is 4:00 P.M. (MST) & Check out time is 12:00 PM. (MST)

Every effort will be made to accommodate early arrivals, as rooms become available.

Question

Is there a dress code?

Answer

Nope! Wedding will be cocktail/dressy casual but please wear what you are comfortable in as the ceremony will be held on the beach!

Question

Can I make a special request for my dinner at the reception?

Answer

Yes, please let Bri know of any allergies!

Question

Are we able to go to the sister resorts (Pueblo Bonito Los Cabos and Pueblo Bonito Rose)?

Answer

All inclusive privileges are valid under the plan of the sister properties including Pueblo Bonito Rose and Pueblo Bonito Los Cabos including private access to swimmable beaches. Guests may use the facilities, restaurants and bars as per the All-Inclusive program. Complimentary shuttle between sister resorts will run every hour up until 10:00PM.

Question

So what does the guest room rate include?

Answer

Hotel accommodations; breakfast, lunch and dinner at any on site restaurants (besides La Frida at Pueblo Bonito Sunset Beach), alcoholic and nonalcoholic international brands served at restaurants and bars, programmed hotel theme nights, selected daily activities.

Question

What isn't included in the All-Inclusive package at Pueblo Bonito Sunset Beach?

Answer

Items purchased at the Deli Bistro, coffee shop, tobacco shop, gift shop and jewelry store; spa treatments; golf or products at Quivira Club, special liquors, laundry and dry cleaning.

Question

Is there a surcharge at any of the restaurants?

Answer

Yes, at Pueblo Bonito Sunset Beach La Frida is currently a $30 usd per person surcharge and Steakhouse by Quivira Golf Club is currently $35 per person surcharge.

Question

What is the cancellation policy?

Answer

No charges if your cancellation is made 21 days prior to your arrival. Cancellation must be made before 5:00pm MST and a written cancellation confirmation must be received. Individual guests room cancellations made from 20 to 0 days, prior to guest arrival, will be charged 1 night stay at the group rate.
